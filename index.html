*The Lion King* is a timeless Disney classic that follows the journey of a young lion named Simba. After the tragic loss of his father, Simba must learn what it means to grow up, take responsibility, and find his place in the Circle of Life. With breathtaking animation, unforgettable characters, and a story that touches the heart, *The Lion King* continues to inspire audiences of all ages.
